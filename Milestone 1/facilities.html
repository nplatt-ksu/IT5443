Facilities

The Robin and Doug Shore Innovation Center has several cutting edge facilites that faculty can utilize.

Clean Room

The Innovation Center has KSU's first-ever clean room that faculty can use to advance their research. The clean room is equipped with state-of-the-art filtration systems and climate control to ensure optimal conditions for sensitive research.

Numerous Labs

The Innovation Center has labs on all three floors of the building.

  • Floor One: Metal Lab for Welding, Maker Space Lab
  • Floor Two: Dry Lab, Cyber Lab
  • Floor Three: Wet Lab, Teaching Labs

Classrooms

The Innovation Center also has two classrooms (one 120-student and one 60-student). Each classroom is equipped with the latest technology to enhance the learning experience. The classrooms can also be reserved for events and meetings.
